Pricing

At Side Quests we have pricing that varies depending on our projected capacity. Below is a table that shows the price depending on the day and time. Our low hours are from 12pm to 4pm, with peak hours being from 5pm to closing. You can only reserve setups for a maximum amount of time in order to let others have a chance to use them. If a session reaches its maximum time and no one else has reserved it, the session can be extended. Maximum time for a session is four hours unless specified on pricing chart. A session must be at least an hour unless otherwise specified on the pricing chart.

We also offer student discounts and lower rates for large reservations. To arrange large reservations please call at least 48 hours in advance.
