Who We Are

Located at 39 Eden Street, we're home to the largest collection of both modern and retro gaming in the province!
Whether you want to sit down and play some good old-fashioned couch co-op or even just sit down and enjoy a single-player game you haven't gotten a chance to try before, we're here for you.
With a collection of both classic and modern consoles as well as VR setups, we hope to provide access to as much quality gaming as possible. If that's not your style though, we're also home to a large selection of both classic and modern board games.
If you're the type to enjoy a snack between games, or maybe you fancy a drink while you're playing we provide snacks all day long with a bar that opens up in the evening.
